Guitar Reflection

I spent 125 hours practicing guitar. I improved my timing and chord changes by using a metronome and tracking difficult sections instead of repeating whole songs.

  • Skills developed: consistency, focus, listening, creativity
  • Challenge: getting stuck on hard transitions
  • How I solved it: slow practice + repetition in small sections
  • Next goal: target multiple songs with my band to play on stage.

Tennis Reflection

I spent 50 hours on tennis. I became more consistent by focusing on footwork and keeping rallies going instead of hitting risky shots.

  • Skills developed: discipline, sportsmanship, confidence, and self self-control
  • Challenge: nerves in practice matches
  • How I solved it: breathing routine + using my sense of touch to feel the material of the ball
  • Next goal: improve service accuracy and play 3 match sets as well as play in tournaments.

Fitness Reflection

I spent over 100 hours training. I learned that progress comes from consistency: showing up, tracking workouts, working on form, and improving form gradually.

  • Skills developed: resilience, planning, self-control
  • Challenge: staying consistent when busy
  • How I solved it: shorter workouts + organising on excel
  • Next goal: set a monthly strength target and track it

Coding Reflection

I spent 30 hours learning coding. Even small projects taught me problem-solving: break the task into steps, test often, and fix errors calmly.

  • Skills developed: logical thinking, patience, creativity
  • Challenge: understanding errors and debugging
  • How I solved it: read error messages + search examples + try small changes
  • Next goal: Build an ai integrated camera system that can detect and track when you get distracted in real time
